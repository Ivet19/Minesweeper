Minesweeper

Game instructions

The goal of the game is to uncover all the empty cells without exploding the mines.

To open a cell just click on it.

When a cell opens, you can see the number of its adjacent mines. This makes it easier to deduce which cells are safe around.

If the cell you open contains a mine, you lose the game.

The counter indicates the total number of mines on the board.

Good luck!
